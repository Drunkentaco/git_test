Image formats

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can.

there is one potential issues with SVGs: for them to display consistently across browsers, you need to convert any text fields to outlines using your image editor (e.g., Abode Illustrator or Sketch). If our images contain a lot of text (like the fancy screenshots in this tutorial), this can have a big impact on file size.
